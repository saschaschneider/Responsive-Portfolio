About Me

Originally from Germany, Sascha worked and studied in 3 different countries. Currently he resides in the sunshine state, in the city of Angels. In 2011 Sascha finished his degree in Management Science from the University of California, San Diego. He then started consulting several German companies on its processes before moving into the startup world. There he first led the operations of an online marketing startup, which we all know what it entails: Sascha did everything, from brewing coffee, cold calling, hiring to also defining strategic operations.

Eventually he was called to Dublin, to help Google build an agency focused brand team. Since November 2012, Google helps him build his career and skills. On one side he advises and supports sales on Google’s Brand Products, mostly YouTube, on the other he provides consulting to the engineering team in order to improve Google’s proprietary brand tools.
